Load Calculations & Power Requirements

Accurate load calculations form the foundation of reliable electrical systems. Our engineers perform detailed load assessments to determine exact power requirements for your project.

Our Expertise:

  • ▸ Residential load calculations for single and multi-unit dwellings
  • ▸ Commercial building electrical demand assessment
  • ▸ Industrial load profiling and power factor analysis
  • ▸ Diversity factor calculations for optimized capacity planning
  • ▸ Future expansion capacity planning

Why It Matters

Undersized systems lead to overloading and failures. Oversized systems waste resources. Our precise calculations ensure your electrical infrastructure is perfectly sized for current and future needs.

Industry Standards

Reliability & Safety

Earthing & Lightning Protection Systems

Safety is paramount. Our earthing and lightning protection systems safeguard buildings, equipment, and lives from electrical hazards and lightning strikes.

Safety Systems We Design:

  • ▸ Earthing pit design and earth electrode sizing
  • ▸ Lightning protection system layout and design
  • ▸ Down conductor and bonding conductor planning
  • ▸ Surge protection device (SPD) selection and placement
  • ▸ Earthing resistance testing and maintenance planning

Standards Compliance

All designs comply with IS 1989, IS 2309, and IEC 61024 standards for earthing and lightning protection. Your facility remains protected in all weather conditions.
